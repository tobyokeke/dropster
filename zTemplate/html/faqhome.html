How do I place an order?

All you have to do is to download our app from either playstore or app store and register and you can start placing orders with our easy to use features..

How long should it take to ship my parcel?

Weather, traffic, road conditions and other unforeseen factors make guaranteeing the delivery time impossible for all delivery companies; however we make sure all deliveries within the city are within the time frame of 30 - 60 minutes. One thing DROPSTER can guarantee is that our team will be working very diligently to make sure your goods are delivered within the 30 -60 minutes time estimate as well as keep you informed every step of the way.

Why do i have to choose a larger vehicle for some goods?

We have specified size dimensions for each delivery vehicle, once your good has exceeded the dimensions for a particular vehicle we strongly recommend that you choose your goods to be delivered with the next available vehicle that fits the dimension of your goods, we also recommend that for very delicate goods which may smudge, break, wear or tear in a particular vehicle, the next safer vehicle should be picked for delivery of such goods

Can i be set up on auto payments?

No, for now you cannot. The closest we have to auto payments is to load up your in-app account using a coupon or your debit/credit card and once you place an order using "balance" we deduct automatically from the balance.

Are my goods insured during transit?

Absolutely! Your goods are fully insured during transit by general carrier liability insurance. All of our qualified carriers are fully insured.

What should i do if my goods arrive with damage?

New damage on your goods can be a stressful process. DROPSTER makes it easy to open and settle a damage claim with our qualified carrier. During the drop off inspection process, call immediately and ask to speak with our support department who will instruct you further. If our support team is not available, notate all new damage on the issues form in the app and we will respond promptly..

How do i track my goods?

DROPSTER comes with a unique feature of live tracking your goods through a map as it makes its transition from pick up to drop off, so at any point you know where your good is.

What if i am not available at the time the good is to be delivered?

We recommend you try your best to receive the order, as a thorough inspection of the good for damage must be completed. If you are not able to receive your good, an authorized party may complete the drop-off inspection process on your behalf.

What payment options do i have?

We gladly accept any one of the following payment methods: credit/debit cards to top-up the in-app balance, credit/debit cards for POS transactions, cash payments and Bitcoin..

Requirements for vehicle partner?

If you would like to become a vehicle partner, visit our vehicle partners page, sign up as a vehicle partner and we will get in touch with you, you can also download our vehicle partnership agreement..
